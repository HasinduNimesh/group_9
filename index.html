Welcome to the Department of Computer Science


The Department of Computer Science, founded on the 1st of January 2015, has been dedicated to the noble pursuit of nurturing computer science professionals of international repute. Our mission encompasses a twofold objective: to cater to the burgeoning IT industry's demands and to foster cutting-edge research in the field. As the youngest department within the faculty, we offer a comprehensive array of course modules. These modules delve into the scientific and theoretical aspects of computing, while also keeping a keen eye on the ever-evolving trends in computing, particularly within the domain of Artificial Intelligence.

We take immense pride in presenting two prominent academic programs: the BSc (Hons) in Computer Science and the BSc (Hons) in Software Engineering. These programs are tailored for students with a background in the physical sciences and those who have successfully completed their GCE A/L examinations. Our department's research endeavors span a wide spectrum, delving into the realms of Theoretical Computing and Artificial Intelligence. We are committed to not only enhancing our research culture but also contributing to the larger faculty-wide research landscape. In our quest for excellence, we continue to shape the future of computer science education and research, striving for academic and professional distinction.
